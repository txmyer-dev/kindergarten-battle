KINDERGARTEN SWARM

Inspired by Smosh Pit: "Would you win this battle?"

🚨 THE STIPULATIONS

  • Wave release: 5 aggressive kindergarteners every 30 seconds.
  • Goal: Keep moving and strike them down. If they dog-pile you, shake them off!
  • Defeat: If they pin you to the ground for 15 consecutive seconds, you lose.
  • Weapons: Unarmed, but in Easy/Medium mode you can pick up and throw classroom desks and chairs!
